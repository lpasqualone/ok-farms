OK Farms is a family run farm nestled on five acres close to Ramona, California.

Throughout the year, we provide to our community an abundance of naturally grown fruits and vegetables. We're also home to a number of small animals including chickens, turkeys, geese, an ever-growing tribe of goats, and of course our beloved dogs Sedona and Nala.
