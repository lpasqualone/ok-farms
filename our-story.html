A little bit about us

OK Farms got its start a few years ago in 2020. In the midst of everything, we bought a home just outside of Ramona, and got to work on a few projects (read: *a lot of projects*). Starting from the ground up, we built a chicken coop, found a space to raise some goats, and started tilling the ground for an extra-large vegetable garden. Ever since then we've been on this little adventure into the process of raising crops and animals.

We are loving the reward that comes from growing your own food, and getting to share that with others! We're also passionate about building community, and providing support where needs have not yet fully been met.
